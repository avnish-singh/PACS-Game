HOW TO PLAY

01

Connect Nodes

Drag from an output port (►) to an input port to draw a data route between imaging nodes.

02

Fix Config Errors

Click misconfigured nodes to open their config panel. Enter correct IPs, ports, and AE Titles.

03

Route Studies

Studies appear in the patient queue. Drag them to the correct destination storage or viewer.

04

Manage Storage

Move studies between PACS, Archive, and Cloud before storage capacity reaches 100%.

05

Beat the Timer

Each level has a countdown. Incomplete workflows cause study failures and score penalties.

06

Emergency Mode

Random STAT cases arrive with a 30-second critical timer. Prioritize them for bonus points.
